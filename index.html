How a Dual-Income Couple Built ₹35 Lakhs in Just 5 Years

₹35 Lakhs
Corpus built in 5 years via SIP discipline
The Situation

Rahul & Priya, both working professionals, were saving in FDs with 5.5% returns — barely beating inflation. They had no investment plan and feared market risk.

The Strategy

We designed a ₹15,000/month SIP across 3 funds — large-cap, mid-cap, and flexi-cap — aligned to their 5-year home-buying goal. Monthly rebalancing guidance via WhatsApp.

The Outcome

How a Gujarati Business Owner Secured His Child's Entire Future

₹48 Lakhs
Education corpus built in 8 years
The Situation

Vikram bhai, a textile trader from Surat, wanted to fund his daughter's medical studies but had inconsistent income and no structured savings plan.

The Strategy

We designed a variable SIP model — higher investment in peak business months, lower in off-season — linked to a dedicated education goal fund with ELSS tax benefits.

The Outcome

₹48 lakhs ready when his daughter turned 18. Full medical college fees covered. Vikram bhai calls it "the best business decision I ever made."
